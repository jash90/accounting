AI-powered accounting intelligence for the modern Polish market. Our visual identity bridges traditional accounting reliability with innovative AI capabilities, creating trust through smart automation.

01 — Brand Foundation

Brand Essence

AppTax represents the evolution of accounting—where human expertise meets artificial intelligence to create a seamless, trustworthy experience for modern accounting professionals.

Mission

To empower Polish accounting professionals with AI-driven tools that enhance efficiency, ensure compliance, and enable proactive client service—transforming how accounting firms operate.

Vision

Become the leading AI-powered accounting platform in Poland, setting the standard for intelligent automation while maintaining the trust and reliability the industry demands.

Brand Personality

Intelligent — Powered by advanced AI
Trustworthy — Professional and reliable
Progressive — Forward-thinking innovation
Approachable — Complex made simple

Target Audience

Tech-savvy accounting professionals and firms in Poland seeking efficiency gains through automation. They value innovation but require the credibility and compliance assurance essential in their industry.

07 — Guidelines

Usage Guidelines

Maintain brand consistency by following these essential do's and don'ts.

Do

  • Use the logo with adequate clear space
  • Maintain color consistency across applications
  • Use Inter typeface for all communications
  • Apply the AI indicator (teal glow) for smart features
  • Ensure sufficient contrast for accessibility
  • Scale the logo proportionally
  • Use approved color combinations
  • Keep messaging professional yet approachable

Don't

  • Distort or stretch the logo
  • Use unapproved colors
  • Add effects like drop shadows to the logo
  • Place the logo on busy backgrounds
  • Modify the logo's proportions
  • Use the logo smaller than minimum size (24px height)
  • Rotate or flip the logo
  • Use low contrast color combinations
