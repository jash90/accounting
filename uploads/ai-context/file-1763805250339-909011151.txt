Tax Deduction Information 2024

Office Expenses:
- Office supplies: Up to $500 per month
- Software subscriptions: 100% deductible  
- Internet and phone: 50% deductible if home office

Travel Expenses:
- Mileage: $0.67 per mile for business travel
- Hotel stays: 100% deductible for business trips
- Meals: 50% deductible

Employee Benefits:
- Health insurance: 100% deductible
- Retirement contributions: Up to $66,000 per employee
- Training and education: 100% deductible
